The most popular questions

How can I order if our address is not in your delivery area?

If your address is outside our delivery area, you can:

Pick up the order by pickup at one of our addresses. (In this case, you will receive "Baked mono maki with salmon as a gift).

Find out from the operator whether it is possible to make an exception for delivery outside the zone at this time.

How long to wait for the order?

Delivery time depends on the order address.

Delivery to the green zone - up to 1 hour.

Delivery to the yellow zone - up to 1.5 hours.

Is shipping free?

Yes, delivery is free with a minimum order amount of UAH 200 in the green delivery zone and UAH 300 in the yellow zone.

What time do you accept orders?

We accept orders every day from 11:00 a.m. to 9:30 p.m. (in connection with the curfew)
